Stage 3-4: slow-wave detection + dissipation curves (+ parallel download)

slow_waves.py: yasa.sw_detect per subject on concatenated NREM epochs; records
each wave's negative slope (SHY proxy) and real night time via epoch_idx.
dissipation.py: bin slopes into 12 temporal bins x channel; dissipation rate =
fractional decline of channel-averaged curve; median-split fast/slow label.
download.py: Sleep-EDF download now parallel (PhysioNet throttles ~0.15 MB/s per
connection); idempotent + resumable.

First real result (DREAMS Patients, n=27): the cohort-average dissipation curve
is flat with a slight UPWARD tilt (early bins 0.997 vs late 1.047; paired
t=-1.40, p=0.17). Slow-wave slope does NOT decline across the night as SHY
predicts in this cohort. Most likely confound: DREAMS Patients have sleep
pathologies. Pulling the healthy DREAMS Subjects DB to test SHY on clean sleepers.
